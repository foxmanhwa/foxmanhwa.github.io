# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Mixed audience with no single profile: anime/manhwa fans who found the YouTube channel, developers who came from PresencePal or Manhwa Pulse, curious visitors following a GitHub link, and friends/mutuals who already know Milo. No single job to be done; the visit itself is the experience. Primary context is casual browsing, not task completion.

## Product Purpose

A personal home on the web for Milo54 — reader, builder, professional manhwa addict. The site is a living snapshot of who Milo is right now: what they're reading, what they've built, what they're into. Success means a visitor leaves feeling like they spent time in a friend's room — warm, a little chaotic, full of personality. Not a portfolio deck, not a fan wiki. A place.

## Brand Personality

Voice: direct, enthusiastic, self-aware without being ironic. Tone: like a good Discord message from a friend — casual but considered, never performative.

Three words: cozy, handcrafted, quietly ambitious.

## Anti-references

- **Slick SaaS portfolio**: dark-mode glass cards, "I build scalable solutions" copy, hero metrics, identical card grids.
- **Anime fan wiki**: information-dense tables, functional-but-joyless layouts, zero spatial personality.
- **Corporate clean**: white space for its own sake, neutral type, LinkedIn energy.

The site should feel built by hand, not assembled from a template.

## Design Principles

1. **Lived-in over polished.** Every section should feel placed by a person, not laid out by a grid system. Slight asymmetry and deliberate imperfection are features, not bugs.
2. **Show the person, not the resume.** What Milo is reading right now is as important as the projects. Don't let the portfolio section overshadow the personality.
3. **Quiet competence.** The projects exist and speak for themselves. No hero metrics, no "built for X users" copy. Let the work land without announcement.
4. **Cozy, not cloying.** Pink and handwritten fonts should feel warm, not saccharine. Every decorative element earns its place — decoration that doesn't serve the warmth gets cut.
5. **Accessible by default.** Full WCAG AA is not optional. The cozy aesthetic includes everyone.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Full WCAG AA compliance. Text contrast on all interactive and body elements. Decorative images marked aria-hidden. prefers-reduced-motion respected for all animations. Screen-reader coverage on navigation and content landmarks.
